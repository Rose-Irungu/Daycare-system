Meet Sarah

About Sarah

Sarah has been working with children for over 5 years. She is highly experienced in managing the needs of young children and ensuring their safety while fostering a caring and playful environment. Sarah's approach is based on building strong relationships with children through activities that are both fun and educational.

Specialties

  • Outdoor play and games
  • Creative arts and crafts
  • Certified in child first aid and CPR

Experience

Sarah has worked with children ranging from infants to school-age, ensuring their physical and emotional well-being at all times. She creates custom activities based on each child's age and interests to promote healthy development.

Availability

Sarah is available for both full-time and part-time babysitting services. She can accommodate flexible schedules to meet your needs.
